**No voiceover.** Optional: soft, ambient instrumental (meditation/spa style) or silence. No loud or tacky music.

**Output:** Either generate the 8-second video, or give a detailed shot-by-shot storyboard with timings and exact text for each frame so I can produce it in a video editor.
