## Conversion Tips

1. **Free trial** – 3–7 days (like Astrology.com, Hint) to reduce friction.
2. **Introductory offer** – 50% off first month for new users.
3. **Yearly discount** – Emphasize savings vs monthly (e.g. 2 months free).
4. **Feature teasers** – Show locked features with “Subscribe to unlock” CTA.
5. **Regional pricing** – Use Play Console country pricing for local affordability.
