Version 1.2.5 – What's new

• Stability & reliability – Fixed app launch issues for a smooth startup experience on all devices.
• Rewarded ads – Watch ads to unlock chat messages, earn Karma points, and access premium features. Rewarded ads now load and work correctly.
• Yoga Lab improvements – Fixed alignment of Grah (planet), House, and Rashi selectors. Added optional third planet for advanced conjunction analysis.
• Ad experience – Banner, full-page, and native ads now display properly. Improved ad loading and error handling.
• Save & share – Save Kundali reports, horoscopes, and Panchang to your device. Share content easily with friends and family.
• Performance – General bug fixes and optimizations for a faster, more reliable experience.
