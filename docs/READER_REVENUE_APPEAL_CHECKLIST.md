# Reader Revenue Manager – Appeal Checklist

Use this checklist before submitting your appeal so reviewers can verify compliance.

## 1. Site access & ownership

- [ ] **Verify ownership** of `https://www.cosmicjyoti.com` in Publisher Center (or Search Console). Add the verification meta tag in `index.html` when Google provides it (see comment in `<head>`).
- [ ] Confirm the site loads in an **incognito window** with no login wall, no geo-blocking, and no certificate errors.
- [ ] Ensure **robots.txt** allows crawlers: `Allow: /` is set; only `/api/` is disallowed.

## 2. Disclaimers (implemented)

- [x] **Initial HTML** – A disclaimer bar appears in the first HTML response (before React), so crawlers see it.
- [x] **App hub** – A disclaimer notice is shown on the main hub with links to Terms and Privacy.
- [x] **Footer** – Full “Important Disclaimer” section in the app footer (EN/Hindi).
- [x] **Terms of Service** – Section 4 includes: entertainment/education only, no guaranteed outcomes, remedies for cultural/reflective purposes only, no professional advice, user acknowledgment.
- [x] **Privacy Policy** – “Nature of service” paragraph with disclaimer and link to Terms.
- [x] **About page** – Disclaimer includes “No outcomes guaranteed” and remedies framing.

## 3. Content framing

- Content is framed as **entertainment and education only**.
- **No guaranteed outcomes** stated in disclaimers and Terms.
- **Remedies / upay** described as cultural and reflective, not as cures or substitutes for professional advice.
- **No misleading previews** – headlines and descriptions match the actual content.

## 4. When submitting the appeal

In your appeal message, you can briefly state:

1. **Ownership** – “I have verified (or am verifying) ownership of the root URL https://www.cosmicjyoti.com.”
2. **Access** – “The site is publicly accessible with no login required; our robots.txt allows crawling of the main site.”
3. **Policies** – “We have reviewed the Reader Revenue Manager Content Policies and implemented clear disclaimers site-wide: content is for entertainment and educational purposes only, no outcomes are guaranteed, and we do not present remedies as substitutes for professional advice. Disclaimers appear in the initial HTML, on the main hub, in the footer, and in our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.”

Keep the appeal short and factual. You have a limited number of appeals, so submit only after the above steps are done.
