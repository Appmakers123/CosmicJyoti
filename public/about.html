About CosmicJyoti

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Our Mission

CosmicJyoti is dedicated to making the ancient wisdom of Vedic Astrology accessible to everyone in the modern world. We combine traditional astrological principles with cutting-edge AI technology to provide accurate, personalized insights that help you understand your destiny, make informed decisions, and navigate life's challenges with confidence.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between ancient Vedic knowledge and contemporary seekers, offering a comprehensive platform that respects tradition while embracing innovation.

Who We Are

Based in Mumbai, India, CosmicJyoti is a team of passionate astrologers, software developers, and spiritual guides committed to bringing authentic Vedic Astrology to a global audience.

Our team combines:

  • Traditional Expertise: Deep knowledge of Vedic astrology, Panchang, Muhurat, and ancient Indian spiritual practices
  • Modern Technology: AI-powered calculations and personalized predictions
  • User-Centric Design: Intuitive interfaces that make complex astrological concepts easy to understand
