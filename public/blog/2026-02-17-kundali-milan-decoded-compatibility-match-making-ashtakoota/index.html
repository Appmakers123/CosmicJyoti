Kundali Milan Decoded: Mastering Compatibility and Match Making with Ashtakoota

Published: 17 February 2026 at 6:00 am | By Nikesh Maurya | 8 min

Vedic astrology views marriage as a sacred union of souls, guided by the stars. Kundali Milan, or horoscope matching, uses Ashtakoota to score compatibility out of 36 points, offering insights into emotional, physical, and spiritual synergy between partners.

Understanding Kundali Milan: The Foundation of Vedic Match Making

Rooted in ancient texts like Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, this method compares birth charts to predict marital success. A score above 18 is acceptable, 24+ ideal, and 32+ exceptional.

The Role of Birth Charts in Compatibility

Each partner's Kundali—detailing planets, houses, and dashas—reveals innate traits. The 7th house (marriage) and Venus (love) are pivotal.

Ashtakoota: The Eight Pillars of Compatibility

Ashtakoota breaks compatibility into eight quantifiable factors, each with maximum points and ruling elements.

Varna: Spiritual Compatibility (1 Point)

Assesses ego and temperament hierarchy—Brahmin (highest) to Shudra. Matches within or higher Varna score full points.

Vashya: Mutual Attraction (2 Points)

Classifies signs into groups like Chatushpada (quadrupeds). Controlling or friendly Vashya ensures desire and control balance.

Tara: Health and Prosperity (3 Points)

Counts nakshatras from bride's to groom's Moon, avoiding harmful Taras like Nadi for longevity.

Yoni: Physical and Sexual Harmony (4 Points)

27 Yonis (animal symbols) gauge intimacy—compatible pairs like Horse-Horse thrive.

Graha Maitri: Mental Rapport (5 Points)

Moon sign lords' friendship—friendly planets foster understanding.

Gana: Temperamental Match (6 Points)

Deva, Manushya, Rakshasa Ganas—similar Gana prevents clashes.

Bhakoot: Emotional and Financial Stability (7 Points)

Moon sign positions—certain combinations like 2/12 indicate wealth flow.

Nadi: Health and Genetics (8 Points)

Different Nadis (Adi, Madhya, Antya) prevent progeny issues.

Beyond Ashtakoota: Advanced Compatibility Checks

While Ashtakoota is primary, Mangal Dosha (Mars in 1,4,7,8,12), Nadi Dosha remedies, and Dasha alignment provide deeper layers.

Step-by-Step Match Making Process

  1. Generate accurate birth charts with date, time, place.

  2. Compute Ashtakoota scores.

  3. Check doshas and planetary strengths.

  4. Analyze 7th lord placements and Venus-Mars aspects.

  5. Consult for remedies if scores are borderline.

Practical Applications and Real-Life Examples

For a Leo Moon groom and Cancer Moon bride: High Graha Maitri but check Bhakoot. Remedies like kumbh vivah neutralize doshas.

Remedies for Low Compatibility

  • Mangal Dosha: Worship Lord Hanuman.

  • Nadi Dosha: Silver elephant exchange.

  • Perform Graha Shanti puja.

Common Myths and FAQs on Match Making

Is 36/36 mandatory? No, quality over quantity—focus on Nadi and Bhakoot.

Love marriages viable? Yes, with strong Venus and 5th house synergy.
