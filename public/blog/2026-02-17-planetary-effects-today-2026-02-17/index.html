The Solar Eclipse's Universal Impact

Today's annular solar eclipse in Aquarius, with Sun-Moon-Mercury-Venus-Rahu conjunction, ushers transformation across rashis. Dhanishta nakshatra brings volatility yet opportunity, especially in finances and spirituality. No Sutak in India, but global energies shift destinies[5][7].

Effects on Air Signs: Mithuna, Tula, Kumbha

  • Mithuna (Gemini): Communication flows; avoid rudeness with kin. Good sibling time[5].
  • Tula (Libra): Venus-Rahu transforms relations; self-control key.
