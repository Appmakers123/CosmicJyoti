Kundali Milan: Unveiling Relationship Compatibility Through Vedic Astrology

Published: 22 February 2026 at 6:00 am | By Nikesh Maurya | 8 min

Kundali Milan: Understanding Relationship Compatibility

In Vedic astrology, Kundali Milan, also known as horoscope matching, is a traditional practice used to assess the compatibility between two individuals before marriage. This system analyzes the positions of planets and stars at the time of birth to determine the potential for a harmonious and successful relationship.

The Significance of Ashtakoota in Kundali Milan

The most widely used method in Kundali Milan is the Ashtakoota Milan system, which evaluates eight different aspects (Kootas) of compatibility, each carrying a specific number of points. The total points achievable are 36, and a score of 18 or more is generally considered a good match.

The Eight Kootas:

  1. Varna: Represents the spiritual compatibility and ego compatibility between partners. (1 point)
  2. Vasya: Indicates the power equation and mutual attraction between the couple. (2 points)
  3. Tara: Signifies health and well-being after marriage. (3 points)
  4. Yoni: Represents sexual compatibility and biological compatibility. (4 points)
  5. Graha Maitri: Indicates mental compatibility, friendship, and mutual understanding. (5 points)
  6. Gana: Represents temperament and behavioral compatibility. (6 points)
  7. Bhakoot: Signifies the influence of planetary positions on financial prosperity and family welfare. (7 points)
  8. Nadi: Represents genetic compatibility and overall health of the couple. (8 points)

Beyond Ashtakoota: Deeper Analysis

While Ashtakoota provides a valuable overview, a comprehensive Kundali Milan involves a deeper analysis of planetary positions, aspects, and doshas (afflictions) in both horoscopes. This includes evaluating the influence of planets like Mars (Mangal Dosha), Saturn, and Rahu/Ketu on the relationship.

Key Considerations:

  • Mangal Dosha: The presence of Mars in certain houses can indicate potential conflicts and challenges in the marriage.
  • Planetary Aspects: The aspects between planets in both charts reveal the nature of the relationship dynamics.
  • Dasha Analysis: Examining the planetary periods (Dashas) can provide insights into the timing of significant events in the relationship.

Benefits of Kundali Milan

Kundali Milan offers several benefits for couples considering marriage:

  • Identifying Potential Challenges: It helps identify potential areas of conflict and challenges that may arise in the relationship.
  • Understanding Compatibility: It provides insights into the couple's compatibility on various levels, including emotional, intellectual, and physical.
  • Finding Remedial Measures: It suggests remedial measures to mitigate the negative influences of planetary positions and doshas.
  • Promoting Harmony and Understanding: It helps couples understand each other better and fosters harmony and understanding in their relationship.

Limitations of Kundali Milan

It's important to note that Kundali Milan is not a guarantee of a successful marriage. It is a tool that provides insights and guidance, but ultimately, the success of a relationship depends on the efforts and commitment of both partners. Factors such as individual values, communication skills, and mutual respect play a crucial role in building a strong and lasting relationship.
