Planetary Effects on You Today: A Zodiac Sign Forecast (February 22, 2026)

Today, February 22, 2026, the celestial landscape is painted with a unique arrangement of planets, each exerting its influence on the 12 zodiac signs (rashis) in Vedic astrology. Let's explore how these planetary positions may affect you based on your Moon sign (the sign where the Moon was located at the time of your birth).

Aries (Mesh Rashi)

With the Moon in your third house, communication and short journeys are highlighted. Be mindful of impulsive decisions. Mars in Capricorn aspects your tenth house, boosting your career prospects. A good day to focus on professional goals. Remedy: Offer water to the Sun in the morning.

Taurus (Vrishabh Rashi)

The Moon in your second house emphasizes finances and family. Be cautious with spending. Jupiter in your sign brings opportunities for growth and expansion. Focus on long-term investments. Remedy: Donate sweets to the needy.

Gemini (Mithun Rashi)

With the Moon in your sign, you may feel emotionally sensitive. Focus on self-care and avoid overthinking. Mercury in your ninth house brings intellectual pursuits and spiritual insights. Remedy: Chant the Gayatri Mantra.

Cancer (Kark Rashi)

The Moon in your twelfth house suggests a need for solitude and introspection. Meditation and spiritual practices are favored. Venus in Pisces aspects your ninth house, bringing good fortune through travel or higher learning. Remedy: Offer milk to Lord Shiva.

Leo (Simha Rashi)

The Moon in your eleventh house brings social connections and networking opportunities. A good day to connect with friends and colleagues. Rahu in your sign may create some confusion or illusions. Stay grounded. Remedy: Donate red lentils to the poor.

Virgo (Kanya Rashi)

The Moon in your tenth house highlights career and public image. Focus on your professional responsibilities. Saturn in your sixth house brings challenges related to health and daily routines. Maintain a healthy lifestyle. Remedy: Feed birds with grains.

Libra (Tula Rashi)

The Moon in your ninth house brings good fortune and spiritual insights. A good day for travel or religious activities. Mars in Capricorn aspects your fourth house, creating some domestic stress. Be patient with family members. Remedy: Offer white flowers to Goddess Lakshmi.

Scorpio (Vrishchik Rashi)

The Moon in your eighth house may bring some emotional turbulence. Practice mindfulness and avoid risky situations. Jupiter in your seventh house brings harmony in relationships. Focus on strengthening your bonds. Remedy: Donate jaggery to a temple.

Sagittarius (Dhanu Rashi)

The Moon in your seventh house emphasizes partnerships and relationships. A good day to spend time with your spouse or business partner. Mercury in your third house enhances communication skills. Remedy: Chant the Hanuman Chalisa.

Capricorn (Makar Rashi)

The Moon in your sixth house brings challenges related to health and daily routines. Prioritize self-care and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Mars in your sign boosts your energy and drive. Focus on achieving your goals. Remedy: Offer sesame seeds to Lord Shani.

Aquarius (Kumbh Rashi)

The Moon in your fifth house brings creativity and romance. A good day for artistic pursuits or spending time with loved ones. Saturn in your sign emphasizes responsibility and discipline. Focus on long-term planning. Remedy: Donate black clothes to the needy.

Pisces (Meen Rashi)

The Moon in your fourth house emphasizes home and family. Spend time with loved ones and create a comfortable environment. Venus in your sign enhances your charm and attractiveness. Remedy: Offer yellow flowers to Lord Vishnu.

General Remedies for the Day

  • Chant mantras dedicated to your ruling planet.
  • Perform acts of charity and kindness.
  • Practice meditation and mindfulness.
  • Spend time in nature.

Remember that these are general interpretations, and the actual effects may vary depending on your individual birth chart. For a more personalized reading, consult with a Vedic astrologer.
