Major Transits and Their Meanings

A transit occurs when a planet moves from one zodiac sign to another. Major transits, especially those involving slower-moving planets like Jupiter and Saturn, can have significant long-term effects.

Jupiter in Taurus

Jupiter's transit in Taurus emphasizes financial stability, material comfort, and a focus on practical matters. This transit is favorable for investments, real estate, and building a secure foundation.

Saturn in Aquarius

Saturn's transit in Aquarius highlights social responsibility, innovation, and a focus on collective well-being. This transit encourages us to work towards a better future for all and to embrace new technologies and ideas.
