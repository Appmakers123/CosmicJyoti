Auspicious Events and Muhurat for March 10, 2026: Best Times for Important Activities

Published: 10 March 2026 at 6:00 am | By Nikesh Maurya | 7 min

Auspicious Events and Muhurat for March 10, 2026

March 10, 2026, presents a unique blend of cosmic energies. Understanding the prevailing tithi, nakshatra, and planetary positions can help you align your activities with the most auspicious times, enhancing the likelihood of success and minimizing potential obstacles. This guide provides insights into the favorable muhurat and what to do or avoid on this day according to Vedic astrology.

Understanding the Panchang for March 10, 2026

The Panchang, the Vedic calendar, is the foundation for determining auspicious timings. It considers five key elements: Tithi (lunar day), Nakshatra (lunar mansion), Yoga (planetary combination), Karana (half lunar day), and Vara (weekday). Let's break down the Panchang for March 10, 2026:

  • Tithi: Consult the Panchang to determine the specific Tithi. Different Tithis are associated with varying degrees of auspiciousness. Some are favorable for starting new ventures, while others are better suited for concluding existing projects.
  • Nakshatra: The Nakshatra ruling on March 10, 2026, significantly influences the day's energy. Each Nakshatra has its own deity, characteristics, and associated activities. For example, Ashwini is good for initiating new things, while Rohini is favorable for marriage and agriculture.
  • Yoga: The Yoga is the sum of the longitudes of the Sun and Moon. Certain Yogas are considered highly auspicious, such as Siddhi Yoga and Amrita Yoga, while others, like Vishkumbha Yoga, are best avoided for important tasks.
  • Karana: Each Tithi is divided into two Karanas. Understanding the Karana can provide further refinement in determining the auspiciousness of a particular time.
  • Vara: The weekday, in this case, Tuesday, also carries its own significance.

Auspicious Muhurat on March 10, 2026

Abhijit Muhurat

The Abhijit Muhurat is generally considered one of the most auspicious times of the day. It typically occurs around midday. Check the specific timings for March 10, 2026, in your location. This muhurat is excellent for starting new ventures, signing contracts, and undertaking important tasks.

Amrita Siddhi Yoga

If Amrita Siddhi Yoga is present on March 10, 2026, it is an exceptionally auspicious time for initiating any activity. This yoga combines a favorable Nakshatra and Vara (weekday) to create a powerful positive influence.

Other Favorable Yogas

Consult the Panchang to identify other auspicious yogas present on March 10, 2026, such as Ravi Yoga or Sarvartha Siddhi Yoga. These yogas can enhance the success of activities undertaken during their duration.

Activities to Favor and Avoid on March 10, 2026

Based on the Panchang and prevailing planetary positions, certain activities are more favorable than others on March 10, 2026:

  • Favorable Activities: Starting new projects (especially during Abhijit Muhurat or Amrita Siddhi Yoga), engaging in charitable activities, performing religious rituals, and focusing on self-improvement.
  • Activities to Avoid: Important travel during Rahu Kalam (an inauspicious period), signing critical documents during unfavorable yogas, and engaging in conflicts or arguments.

Remedies and Practices for March 10, 2026

Even if the day presents some challenges, you can mitigate negative influences through specific remedies and practices:

  • Chanting Mantras: Reciting mantras associated with the ruling deities of the Tithi and Nakshatra can invoke their blessings and enhance positive energies.
  • Donations: Giving to charity, especially to those in need, can help alleviate negative karma and create a more favorable environment.
  • Meditation and Prayer: Engaging in meditation and prayer can help you connect with your inner self and cultivate a sense of peace and well-being.

By understanding the Panchang and aligning your activities with the auspicious timings on March 10, 2026, you can maximize your potential for success and well-being. Remember to consult a qualified Vedic astrologer for personalized guidance.
