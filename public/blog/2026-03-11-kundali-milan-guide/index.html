Unlocking the Secrets of Kundali Milan: A Guide to Compatibility and Match Making

Published: 11 March 2026 at 6:00 am | By Nikesh Maurya | 6 min

Introduction to Kundali Milan

Kundali Milan, or Ashtakoota, is a traditional Vedic astrology method used to assess the compatibility between two individuals. This ancient technique takes into account the positions of the planets and stars at the time of an individual's birth, providing a comprehensive understanding of their personality, strengths, and weaknesses.

Understanding Ashtakoota

The Eight Gunas

Ashtakoota is based on the concept of eight gunas, or qualities, which are used to evaluate the compatibility between two individuals. These gunas include Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakoot, and Nadi. Each guna carries a specific weightage, and the total score is used to determine the overall compatibility between the couple.

Calculating the Score

The Ashtakoota score is calculated by assigning points to each guna, based on the compatibility between the two individuals. The maximum score is 36, and the higher the score, the better the compatibility. A score of 28 or above is considered excellent, while a score below 18 indicates a low level of compatibility.

Match Making and Relationship Compatibility

Kundali Milan is not just about match making; it's also about understanding the dynamics of a relationship and identifying potential areas of conflict. By analyzing the birth charts of the couple, astrologers can provide valuable insights into their strengths, weaknesses, and compatibility.

Tips for a Successful Match

  • Consider the Ashtakoota score and the individual gunas to determine the overall compatibility.
  • Look for a balance between the masculine and feminine energies in the relationship.
  • Pay attention to the positions of the planets, particularly the Moon and Venus, which play a crucial role in emotional compatibility.

Conclusion

Kundali Milan is a powerful tool for determining compatibility and match making. By understanding the intricacies of Ashtakoota and the eight gunas, you can gain a deeper insight into the world of relationships and find your perfect match.
