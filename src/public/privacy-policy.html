Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

CosmicJyoti ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Vedic Astrology application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Birth Details: Name, date of birth, time of birth, and location (for astrological calculations)
  • Account Information: Email address (if provided during login)
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, preferences, and app interactions

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • App usage statistics
  • Location data (only when you provide it for astrological calculations)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate accurate astrological charts and predictions
  • To provide personalized horoscope readings
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you about our services

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your birth chart data locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal birth details to external servers except for:

  • Geocoding services (Google Maps API) to convert location names to coordinates
  • Astrological calculation APIs (only birth date, time, and coordinates - not your name)

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
  • Improve website functionality and performance

5.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to deliver relevant advertisements
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and display preferences

5.2 Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

  • Google AdSense: Uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.
  • Google Analytics: Uses cookies to analyze website traffic and user behavior.

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps API: For location geocoding (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Astrology APIs: For accurate astrological calculations
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements (subject to Google's Privacy Policy and uses cookies for ad personalization)
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (use guest mode)
  • Export your data

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Birth chart data stored locally on your device remains until you delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
